TeX, stylized within the system as TEX, is a typesetting system (or "formatting system") designed and mostly written by Donald Knuth and released in 1978.

"Per Bothner (assistant of Knuth) discusses authorship", Knuth definitely wrote most of the code himself, at least for the Metafont re-write, for which I have pe[r]sonal knowledge. However, some of his students (such as Michael Plass and John Hobby) did work on the algorithms used in TeX and Metafont. https://gcc.gnu.org/ml/java/1999-q2/msg00419.html
